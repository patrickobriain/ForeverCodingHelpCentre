We always love to hear from our users, so if you've got something you'd like to suggest, we want to hear it! You can suggest a tutorial by the following ways:
  1. FEEDBACK WEBSITE - Go here to our feedback website to fill out the feedback form.
  2. EMAIL - You can email us your feedback on feedback@forevercoding.com
  3. LIVE CHAT - You can share your feedback with us via live chat on all our websites.
